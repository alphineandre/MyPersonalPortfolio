About Me

Hello, I'm Alphy Andre Chauke, a passionate software developer with a diploma in Information Technology, specializing in Software Development. Graduating with distinction and an overall average of 83%, I am committed to crafting solutions that are both efficient and innovative.

With a strong foundation in languages such as Java, Kotlin, JavaScript, CSS, HTML, Python, and C#, as well as frameworks like ASP.NET, .NET, and WPF, I'm constantly expanding my technical skills to stay ahead in the tech industry.
